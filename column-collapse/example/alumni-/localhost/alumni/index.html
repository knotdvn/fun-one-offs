National Alumni Association

The Association is an advisory board of alumni elected to promote the general welfare and interests of Syracuse University as an institution with a committment to learning through teaching, research, scholarship, creative accomplishment, and service. Members of the SU Alumni Association Board of Directors are elected based on demonstrated involvement with Syracuse University.  Read the latest blog posted by the SUAA president here.

2013 Orange Circle Awards

Every day, members of the Syracuse University community do great things in the service of others. This year, a highlight of Philanthropy Week was the presentation of the Orange Circle Award to four such individuals: Richard Dulude ’54, Louise Phanstiel, and Melvin T. Stith G’73, G’78 and Patricia L. Stith G’77.

Meet our 2013 recipients in the video above, then learn more about Philanthropy Week, read notes submitted by students during Thank-a-Donor Day, and view photos of the week's events.
